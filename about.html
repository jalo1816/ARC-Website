About Us

Who We Are

Arasu Rubber Corporation Limited is a Government of Tamil Nadu enterprise dedicated to the development of natural rubber plantations and sustainable agricultural growth.

The corporation focuses on plantation management, worker welfare, environmental sustainability, and transparent public service systems across Tamil Nadu.

With decades of experience, the corporation continues to modernize operations and improve productivity while supporting rural employment and eco-friendly initiatives.

Our Mission & Vision

🌿 Sustainability

Promote eco-friendly plantation development and preserve natural resources for future generations.

👨‍🌾 Worker Welfare

Ensure safe working environments and improve the quality of life for plantation workers.

📈 Growth & Innovation

Adopt modern technologies and efficient systems to strengthen rubber production and services.
