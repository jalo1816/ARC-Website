Labour Force

Empowering Rubber Plantation Workers Across Tamil Nadu

Our Workforce

The Corporation has provided employment for around 952 daily paid workers. Among these, 801 are local workers and 99 are repatriates from Sri Lanka. Their primary activities include tapping rubber trees for natural rubber extraction and maintaining plantation areas. In addition, around 400 casual workers are employed based on operational requirements.
